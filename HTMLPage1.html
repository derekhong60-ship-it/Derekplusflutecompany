Audition Information of Derek plus flute

Everyone who's intrested in joining our flute Quartet company always need an audition.

Reserve a audition space by contact Our Member ( names are indicated on the our member section )

Or E-mail: Derekhong60@gmail.com

Audition requirement

Recieve a offer of participation to our audition

Play a Classical Song that can maximum show your skill, Indicate the name and sheet music of the song.

You will need to play some of these scales as a test, they are concert E major, C major, A# minor or D flat major.

No Audition required if you got offer with "No Audition Required".

After Audition. We will contact you for results.
